  • Number of Endings

    There is exactly one ending per letter of the alphabet , although only A-E are the Main endings. The other endings are just for fun, or plain joke endings triggered through mini changes within the storyline.

  • Short Summary of the Main Endings

    Ending A: flowers for m[A]chines, you play as 2B and defeat a villian named Adam, brother of Eve. Ending B: or not to [B]e , you play from 9S' perspective Ending C: meaningless [C]ode you play as 9S , as he learns the truth behind YorHa, and the death of his companion, 2B. He then battles with A2 Ending D: childhoo[D]s end , you play as A2, she kills 2B and fights one final battle against 9S Ending E: [E]nd of YorHa, Yorha ends and all of the androids have been killed, with a mini game that decides a certain path, up to the players discretion. Having the option to delete all saved data and save a random person in the world, or to keep your saved data and do nothing to help.
