A 3rd person RPG game

Nier: Automata is an action role-playing hack and slash video game developed by PlatinumGames and published by Square Enix. The game was released for the PlayStation 4 and Microsoft Windows in early 2017, with an Xbox One port later in June 2018

Characters

2B

2B, a YoRha unit who is accompined by her partner 9S, her mission being to bring "Glory to Mankind"

9S

9S, a YoRha unit who is 2Bs partner, he is a scanner unit that is more defensive than offensive, as a scanner his role differs from 2B, as he finds and analyzes informatio rather than fighting.

A2

A2, the final "Main" YoRha unit, she is an old unit, as defined from her unit letter being first than her unit number.

She is an interesting YoRha unit because unlike her supersets, she is able to speed/power up before self-destructing, Meanwhile any future model simply is left visually and physically imparied
