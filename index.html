A 3rd person RPG game

Nier Automata is an action role-playing hack and slash video game, Taking place in an post-apocalyptic world in where machine lifeforms have taken over with human life at the brink of extinction. YoRha, an elite force of androids created by the Council of Humanity are called on to stage to clear Earth of the machines, and allow for the human race to return back safe. But there is a huge secret to be unraveled.

Characters

2B

, a YoRha unit who is accompined by her partner 9S, her mission being to bring "Glory to Mankind", Although having a secret aliases that comes into play near the final endings of the game.

9S

9S, a YoRha unit who is 2Bs partner, he is a scanner unit that is more defensive than offensive, as a scanner his role differs from 2B, as he finds and analyzes information rather than purely combat.

A2

A2, the final "Main" YoRha unit, she is an old unit, as defined from her unit letter being first than her unit number. She understands the secret behind YoRha, which 9S, and 2B are unaware of.
